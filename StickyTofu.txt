Sauce:
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp sesame oil
2 tbsp ginger
1-2 tbsp brown sugar (play with this)
6-8 cloves garlic
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2-6 ground chili peppers
1/2 cup mirin
2 tbsp rice vinegar
2 tbsp corn starch mixed in 6 tbsp water
black pepper

Made 2 lbs tofu with this sauce, could use a little more volume of sauce for that amount of tofu.

Marinade tofu for at least an hour after pressing.
Fry tofu at high temperature in oil, flipping at a few minutes. Fry until each side is browned and in batches.
Add tofu and vegetables to pot with any extra seasoning, cook until vegetables softened.
Serve over rice or couscous
