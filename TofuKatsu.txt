
1 lb tofu

Katsu Sauce:
1 Tablespoon olive oil
2 cloves garlic crushed or minced and chopped
1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
3 Tablespoons vegan Worcestershire sauce
3 Tablespoons agave nectar
3 Tablespoons soy sauce or Tamari
3 Tablespoons ketchup
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice

Soy Sauce Mixture:
1/4 c soy sauce
1 T mirin

Flour Mixture:
~ 1c flour
1 T garlic powder

Flax Slurry 
3 T flax
6 T water

Breading Mixture:
1 c panko

Bread tofu in soy then flour then flax then breading.
Fry the tofu in high temperature oil covering half of a slab of tofu.

Dip in katsu sauce as eaten.
