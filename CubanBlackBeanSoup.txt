1 lb dried black beans
1 onion chopped
A few more mild hot peppers (poblano, jalapeno, etc.) diced
~1 head of garlic
1/2 c olive oil
Salt and pepper
Chipotle chilies

1/4 c apple cider vinegar (reduce this)

Boil beans and vegetables for 4-5 hours at a simmer, covered. About 20 minutes from the end, add vinegar.
