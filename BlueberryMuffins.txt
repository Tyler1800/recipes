Makes about 24 muffins

600 grams flour
300 grams sugar
30 grams baking powder
4 grams salt

1/2 cup margarine
2 Tbsp vanilla extract
1 cup coconut milk
4 cups blueberries

1 cup unsweetened applesauce or 2 Tbsp ground flax seed and 3/4 cup of water.

Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl.

Incorporate the margarine into the dry ingredients without melting it.

Mix in the wet ingredients. The dough should be wet, but not liquid.

Scoop dough into a muffin tin.

Bake for 35 minutes at 350 F.
