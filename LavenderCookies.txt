Vegan Lavender Cookies:

Ingredients:
1/3 Cup Canola Oil
2/3 Cup Sugar
1/4 Cup Maple Syrup
3 Tbsp. Almond Milk
1 Tbsp. Vanilla Extract

1/2 Tbsp. Flax seed (ground)
2 tbsp Lavender buds
1 tsp baking soda
2 Cups Flour

Mix the first 4 ingredients well by hand with a fork then add vanilla extract and flax seed.

Add the flour and baking soda slowly, mixing with hands once the dough thickens.

Add the lavender buds and mix well. Knead the dough for a few minutes to allow lavender to flavor dough.

Bake at 350 for around 10 minutes on a greased pan.
