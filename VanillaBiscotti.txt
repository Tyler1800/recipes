Makes about 30 biscotti

Ingredients:
4 c - 500 g flour (increase)
4 T - 20 g flax seed
3/4 c - 150 g brown sugar
1.5 c - 300 g milk
4 T - 60 g baking powder
1/2t - 4 g salt
3 T vanilla

Mix wet ingredients into dry ingredients.

Split dough in half and form into two loaves.
Bake at 350 for about 35 minutes.
Remove from the oven and let sit for 5 to 10 minutes before cutting the loaves into pieces and placing them cut-side down onto the baking sheet.
Bake for another 20 to 30 minutes, flipping the biscotti half-way through.
